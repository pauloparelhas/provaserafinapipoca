When an animal changes its body form A LOT as it grows
quando um animal muda muito de forma enquanto cresce

3. Pupa·Pupa / Casulo

What Animals Eat
O que os animais comem
▶
🌱
Herbivore
Herbívoro
▶
Eats ONLY plants, grass, leaves, fruits
Strong flat teeth for chewing plants·dentes planos, estômago forte
Ex: Sheep 🐑, Zebra 🦓, Rabbit 🐰, Turtle 🐢·do material da escola
🥩
Carnivore
Carnívoro
▶
Eats ONLY meat (other animals)
Sharp teeth and claws for hunting·dentes afiados e garras
Ex: Lion 🦁, Bear 🐻, Wolf 🐺, Red Panda 🐼·do material da escola
🍿
Omnivore
Onívoro
▶
Eats BOTH plants AND meat
Can eat a wide variety of foods
Ex: Opossum, Crow 🐦, Pig 🐖, Humans 🧑·do material da escola
