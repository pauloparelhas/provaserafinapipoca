## PART 1: What Animals Eat — Herbivores, Carnivores, Omnivores

### Humans eat food from the food groups!
Humans need different types of food to be healthy and strong. We eat from several food groups.

### The Food Pyramid (what to eat every day):
- **Bottom / Most:** Bread, Cereal, Rice and Pasta — grains give us energy!
- **2nd level:** Vegetables (like carrots, broccoli, spinach) AND Fruits (like apples, bananas, oranges)
- **3rd level:** Milk, Yogurt and Cheese (dairy) + Meat, Poultry, Fish, Eggs, Beans and Nuts (protein)
- **Top / Least:** Fats, Oils and Sweets — only a little!
- **Every day:** Water — 8 glasses a day!

A healthy plate has foods from ALL the food groups.

---

### And animals — what do they eat?

#### HERBIVORE 🌱
**Definition:** An animal that eats ONLY plants — grass, leaves, fruits, and roots.
- The word "herbivore" comes from "herba" (plant in Latin) + "vorare" (to eat)
- Herbivores have strong, flat teeth for chewing plants
- They have strong stomachs to digest tough plant material

**Examples of Herbivores:**
- Sheep 🐑 — eats grass
- Zebra 🦓 — eats grass and leaves
- Rabbit 🐰 — eats carrots and leaves
- Turtle 🐢 — eats plants and algae
- Cow 🐄 — eats grass
- Horse 🐎 — eats grass and hay
- Deer 🦌 — eats leaves and plants
- Giraffe 🦒 — eats leaves from tall trees

#### CARNIVORE 🥩
**Definition:** An animal that eats the meat of other animals.
- Carnivores have SHARP teeth and claws for hunting
- Their bodies are made to catch and eat other animals

**Examples of Carnivores:**
- Lion 🦁 — the "king" of carnivores
- Wolf 🐺 — hunts in packs
- Tiger 🐯 — powerful predator
- Shark 🦈 — ocean predator
- Red Panda 🦝 — eats bamboo shoots and some meat

#### OMNIVORE 🌿🥩
**Definition:** An animal that eats BOTH plants AND meat to survive.
- Omnivores can eat a wide variety of foods
- This helps them survive in many environments

**Examples of Omnivores:**
- Humans 🧑 — we eat vegetables AND meat AND fruits AND grains!
- Pig 🐷 — eats plants and animal scraps
- Crow 🐦‍⬛ — eats seeds, insects, and small animals
- Opossum — eats almost anything
- Bear 🐻 — eats fish, berries, honey, and insects

---

### Key Questions:
❓ What does a herbivore eat? → Only PLANTS
❓ What does a carnivore eat? → Only MEAT (other animals)
❓ What does an omnivore eat? → BOTH plants AND meat
❓ Is a human a herbivore, carnivore or omnivore? → OMNIVORE
❓ What type of teeth does a carnivore have? → Sharp teeth
❓ Give an example of an omnivore → Human, Pig, Bear, Crow

## PART 2: Animal Families — Parents and Their Babies

### Every animal has a family!
Animals have moms and dads too. But in the animal world, we use special names for the male (dad), female (mom), and baby.

---

### 🐴 HORSE FAMILY
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male horse) | **Stallion** |
| Mom (female horse) | **Mare** |
| Baby horse | **Foal** |

**Remember:** A stallion is the daddy horse. A mare is the mommy horse. A foal is the baby horse.

---

### 🐄 CATTLE FAMILY (COW FAMILY)
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male cattle) | **Bull** |
| Mom (female cattle) | **Cow** |
| Baby cattle | **Calf** |

**Remember:** A bull is the daddy cow. A calf is the baby — like in "veal" (vitela)!

---

### 🦁 LION FAMILY
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male lion) | **Lion** |
| Mom (female lion) | **Lioness** |
| Baby lion | **Cub** |

**Remember:** Only the mom is called "Lioness" (with -ess at the end). The baby is a cub!

---

### 🐔 CHICKEN FAMILY
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male chicken) | **Rooster** |
| Mom (female chicken) | **Chicken / Hen** |
| Baby chicken | **Chick** |

**Remember:** The rooster says "cock-a-doodle-doo"! The baby is a chick. 🐥

---

### 🐯 TIGER FAMILY
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male tiger) | **Tiger** |
| Mom (female tiger) | **Tigress** |
| Baby tiger | **Cub** |

**Remember:** Just like the lion, the tiger baby is also called "cub" — and the mommy is "Tigress" (with -ess)!

---

### 🦆 DUCK FAMILY
| Family Member | English Name |
|--------------|-------------|
| Dad (male duck) | **Drake** |
| Mom (female duck) | **Duck** |
| Baby duck | **Duckling** |

**Remember:** The baby duck is a "duckling" — like the story of "The Ugly Duckling"! 🦆

## SUMMARY TABLE — Animal Families

| Animal | Dad | Mom | Baby |
|--------|-----|-----|------|
| Horse | Stallion | Mare | Foal |
| Cattle | Bull | Cow | Calf |
| Lion | Lion | Lioness | Cub |
| Chicken | Rooster | Hen | Chick |
| Tiger | Tiger | Tigress | Cub |
| Duck | Drake | Duck | Duckling |

### Memory tricks:
- **-ess ending = female:** Lioness, Tigress
- **-ling ending = baby:** Duckling
- **Cub = baby of big cats:** Lion cub, Tiger cub
- **Foal rhymes with small** (it IS small — a baby horse!)
